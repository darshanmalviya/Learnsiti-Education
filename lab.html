Science Lab

To meet the requirements of our Senior Middle, Secondary and Senior Secondary students, state-of-the-art labs (for General Science, Math, Physics, Chemistry and Biology) are provided with excellent equipment and facilities , ensuring safe learning experience in a spacious and distinct setup, in concurrence with the CBSE norms.

A STEAM lab is also designed to ensure student success through a combination of text, videos, ICT and real-world, hands-on activities. As against the traditional teacher-centric classroom model, children learn by way of enquiry, exploration, questioning, debate, application, collaboration, challenge
